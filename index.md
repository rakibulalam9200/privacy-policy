## 3. In-App Purchases & Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

Comeback King offers **auto-renewable subscriptions** through the
**Apple App Store In-App Purchase** system.

### Subscription Terms

- All billing and payment is handled securely by **Apple**
- We do **not** collect, store, or process your payment or credit card information
- Subscriptions **automatically renew** unless cancelled at least **24 hours**
  before the end of the current subscription period
- Your **Apple ID account** will be charged for renewal within **24 hours**
  prior to the end of the current period
- You can **manage or cancel** your subscription anytime via:
  **Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions** on your iPhone
- No refunds are provided for the unused portion of an active subscription period

### How to Cancel

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
2. Tap your **Apple ID / Name** at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Select **Comeback King**
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

For refund requests, please contact **Apple Support** directly at:
👉 https://support.apple.com/billing

## 4. Advertising — Google AdMob

We use **Google AdMob** (provided by Google LLC) to display advertisements
within the App. AdMob helps us keep the App free for users.

### Data Collected by AdMob

Google AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve ads:

- Advertising Identifier (IDFA)
- Device type and operating system
- IP address and coarse location
- App usage and interaction data
- Diagnostic information

### App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In compliance with Apple's **App Tracking Transparency (ATT)** framework,
our App will request your permission before accessing your device's
Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for personalized advertising.

- If you **allow tracking**, you may see personalized ads
- If you **deny tracking**, you will see non-personalized ads
- You can change this permission anytime via:
  **Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**

### Opt Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

- **iOS Settings:** Go to **Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**
  and disable tracking for Comeback King
- **Google:** Visit https://adssettings.google.com

For more information on how Google uses data:
👉 https://policies.google.com/privacy
👉 https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
